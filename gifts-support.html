技术支持

最后更新：2024年1月12日

1. 常见问题

会员相关

  • 问：如何购买永久会员？
  • 答：点击App内"会员中心"，选择永久会员选项，通过App Store完成支付。
  • 问：购买会员后没有立即生效怎么办？
  • 答：请尝试以下步骤：
    1. 重启App
    2. 检查App Store订单状态
    3. 恢复购买（点击会员中心的"恢复购买"按钮）

功能相关

  • 问：如何开启节日提醒？
  • 答：在"设置"中找到"提醒设置"，开启相应节日的提醒开关。
  • 问：如何同步数据到其他设备？
  • 答：确保：
    1. 已登录相同的Apple ID
    2. 已开启iCloud同步
    3. 有足够的iCloud存储空间

Technical Support

Last Updated: January 12, 2024

1. FAQ

Membership

  • Q: How to purchase lifetime membership?
  • A: Go to "Membership Center" in the app, select lifetime membership option, complete payment through App Store.
  • Q: What if membership is not activated immediately after purchase?
  • A: Please try the following steps:
    1. Restart the app
    2. Check App Store order status
    3. Restore purchase (click "Restore Purchase" in Membership Center)

Features

  • Q: How to enable holiday reminders?
  • A: Find "Reminder Settings" in "Settings", enable switches for desired holidays.
  • Q: How to sync data to other devices?
  • A: Ensure:
    1. Signed in with the same Apple ID
    2. iCloud sync is enabled
    3. Sufficient iCloud storage space

2. Contact Us

If you encounter other issues:

  • Email: support@gifts.app
  • Feedback: Click "Settings"->"Feedback" in the app
  • Response time: Within 24 hours
